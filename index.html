Our Donation Plans

We are committed to making a lasting impact on the lives of the most vulnerable and excluded children while supporting childrens rights and gender equality. We are also giving poor people foods, medicine, shelter, etc.

Food for a family

Feeding the hungry homeless elderly, women, children, leprosy patients, disabled on the streets as part of zero hunger. Feeding more than 1000 homeless everyday. Eradicating hunger among homeless families who live on the streets. The homeless people get confidence that they will get food daily & not sleep with empty stomach. We made a difference in lives of homeless by giving them a good meal.

Shelter for a family

One of our teams working on the shelter problems of a huge volume of people who dont have any home. Specially for street children and older people we have arranged some shelter center to bring them in the society and make their rehabilitation. We are also providing their basic supplies regularly. We also have some projects to make shelters for the flood affected area.

Education for a child

We have a kids education program which aims at enabling children from particularly vulnerable and underprivileged backgrounds to develop their social and academic potential as a key to happiness in life. Children left vulnerable due to the loss of parents, abuse or extreme deprivation need a lot more support and care to help them achieve their social and academic potential. We are trying to give those children a better way to the future.
